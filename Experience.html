Optimus Prime also known in Japan as Convoy,[a] is a fictional character and the main protagonist of the Transformers franchise. Generally depicted as a brave and noble leader, Optimus Prime is the supreme commander of the Autobots in their fight against the Decepticons. The Transformers characters were developed for the American market after Hasbro representatives visited the 1983 Tokyo Toy Show.[3] Some designs were modified and the coloring was changed; notably, Optimus Prime (released in 1982 as Diaclone No. 17 - Battle Convoy and in 1984 as Powered Convoy) was colored red, chrome, and dark blue. While the Diaclone version was intended to be a piloted mecha, the Transformers version was a sentient robot. The popularity of the Transformers toys resulted in comics, movies, and a TV series.

  1. Leader: He is the wise and just leader of the Autobots, who are in perpetual conflict with the Decepticons.
  2. Transforming vehicle: He famously transforms into a red semi-truck, which allows him to travel, hide, and transport things.
  3. Warrior: He is a powerful and skilled warrior, often considered one of the mightiest Transformers. He is proficient with weapons like his ion blaster and energon axe.
