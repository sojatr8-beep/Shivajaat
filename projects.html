About Me


Optimus Prime is the heroic leader of the Autobots, a transforming robot faction from the planet Cybertron, and a central protagonist of the Transformers franchise. Originally a civilian named Orion Pax, he was chosen to lead after a series of events that led to his transformation into a powerful, wise, and compassionate warrior. He is known for his unwavering dedication to justice, his strong sense of honor, and his famous motto, "Freedom is the right of all sentient beings
